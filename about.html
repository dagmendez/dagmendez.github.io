Today, I'm a Senior Back-end, Data and DevOps Engineer at Xebia. I focus on modeling business domains in Scala so that invalid states are unrepresentable, using the type system to prevent categories of bugs before they occur. My work sits at the intersection of back-end engineering, data platforms, and DevOps, with an emphasis on reliability, correctness, and maintainable design.

I also work on the human side of engineering. I've contributed to improving collaboration and trust between teams, and I regularly write, speak at conferences, and organize meetups. My aim is to share practical approaches that help teams navigate complex data and infrastructure environments.

Before moving into software, I worked in international trade as an Export Advisor and International Trade Analyst. That background, combined with experience in data engineering, gives me a business-oriented perspective on how requirements translate into robust technical solutions.
